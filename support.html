Frequently Asked Questions

How does SchoolMail work?

SchoolMail gives you a unique email address. You set up forwarding from your school emails to this address, and SchoolMail automatically extracts events, dates, and notices so you never miss what matters.

How do I set up email forwarding?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Forwarding Instructions" for step-by-step guides for Gmail, Outlook, and other email providers.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using Google Firebase. Only you can access your data. We do not use analytics or advertising. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Delete Account". This permanently removes your account and all associated data including children, events, and emails.

Why are some events missing or incorrect?

SchoolMail uses AI to extract events from school emails. Some emails may not contain clear event information. You can review and skip any events that don't look right.
